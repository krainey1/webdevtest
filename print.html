Printing

Welcome to printing! In each language there is a different way construct a print statement. In js, you log it into the console using console.log(); Here's what it looks like:

console.log("This is printing");

Notice how there is a semicolon at the end ; and the text is in quotation marks. This is important for later when you work more with variables. The semicolon is also not required in JavaScript, but generally should be used as it's best practice and used in other languages.

There's also different ways you can structure print statements to suit your needs, which we will get to later.
